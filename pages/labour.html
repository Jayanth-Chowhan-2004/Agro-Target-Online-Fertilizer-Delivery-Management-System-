How to Register as a Labourer

Registering on Agro Target is simple and straightforward. Follow these steps to create your profile:

1. Complete the Form

Fill out all the required fields in the registration form with your personal and professional details.

  • Your full name and contact information
  • Your primary agricultural skills
  • Years of experience
  • Your expected daily wage

2. Submit Your Profile

After completing the form, click the "Register Now" button to submit your information.

  • Double-check all entered information
  • Ensure your phone number is correct
  • Provide a clear profile photo if possible

About Agro Target

Revolutionizing agricultural labor connections

Our Mission

Agro Target was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to revolutionize the way farmers and agricultural laborers connect. In rural areas where digital penetration is increasing but traditional hiring methods still dominate, we saw an opportunity to create meaningful change.

"Empowering farmers with workforce solutions and providing fair employment opportunities for agricultural laborers."

The Team

Our team consists of agricultural experts, technologists, and business professionals who are passionate about improving the agricultural ecosystem. We understand the challenges faced by both farmers and laborers, and we're committed to building solutions that address these challenges effectively.

Technology

We leverage cutting-edge web technologies to create a seamless experience for our users. Our platform is designed to work even in low-bandwidth conditions, ensuring accessibility for users in remote rural areas.

Future Plans

We're continuously working to enhance our platform. Future updates will include features like ratings and reviews, advanced search filters, and mobile app versions to make the service even more accessible.
